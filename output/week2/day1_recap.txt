Week 2, Day 1 — Speech Recognition Fundamentals Recap

Welcome to today’s recap! We talked about the fundamentals of Speech-to-Text and Text-to-Speech technologies, which are crucial in allowing AI to understand and produce human speech. We discussed how these technologies face challenges, like distinguishing voices in loud environments and dealing with complex speech patterns. We also covered how to measure the effectiveness of STT models using metrics like Word Error Rate and Character Error Rate, which tell us how accurate these systems are. In TTS, we explored its various applications, from aiding those with disabilities to powering virtual assistants, along with its historical development over the decades. Importantly, we emphasized ethical practices, like obtaining consent for voice cloning and being mindful of privacy. Keep up the great work, and see you next time!
