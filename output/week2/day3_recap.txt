Generate a Podcast from Notes - Recap

Welcome to today’s lesson recap! Today, we focused on how to convert written notes into a brief spoken podcast using language and text-to-speech models. First, the process starts with you gathering your content, whether it's your notes or a lesson transcript. Then, you transform it using a language model to create a clear and engaging spoken script. Finally, you take that script and turn it into audio with a text-to-speech API. It's important to prompt the LLM correctly, ensuring it's designed to sound natural and fits within a certain length. Keep in mind to select a fitting voice for your podcast and keep each episode concise. All of these steps prepare you for the upcoming 'Podcast Studio' project.
