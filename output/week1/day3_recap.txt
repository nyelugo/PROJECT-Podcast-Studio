Recap of Neural Networks & ML Models

Welcome back! Today, we dove into the world of machine learning models and neural networks. First, we learned how to choose the right machine learning models based on whether we have labeled data and if we're working with categories or quantities. Then, we categorized models into distance-based, linear, tree-based, and specialty types, each with unique strengths. We reviewed key evaluation metrics to measure performance, like accuracy and precision for classifications, while RMSE and MAE help us assess our regression models. We stressed the importance of deep learning for unstructured data like images and text, and discussed the best tools for implementation, highlighting Scikit-learn for classic AI and contrasting TensorFlow with Hugging Face for using pre-trained models.
