Recap of Week 1, Day 4 – ML Labs: Data, Training & Evaluation

Welcome back to our daily lesson recap! Today, we dove into some practical labs that brought the concepts from our last lesson to life. First up, we focused on data manipulation, where we learned to load a dataset, clean it, and prepare it for modeling. Remember, a large portion of any project is spent just preparing our data. Next, we moved on to training models with scikit-learn, highlighting the process of splitting our dataset into training and testing sets so we could accurately evaluate our models. We explored various metrics, noting how accuracy alone can be misleading, especially with imbalanced data. We even had the option to build a simple neural network to connect our theoretical knowledge with real coding experience. Overall, the workflow from preparing your data to evaluating your model is key. Keep practicing these steps; great data and honest evaluations will always lead you to better insights. Until tomorrow, keep up the great work!
