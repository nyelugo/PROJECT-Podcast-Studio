Recap of Week 1, Day 5 — Generative AI & LLMs

Welcome back to our daily recap! Today, we delved into Generative AI, focusing on Large Language Models, or LLMs. First, we learned that these models are massive neural networks containing billions of parameters, a big step up from traditional AI systems. We also uncovered how these models use an encoder-decoder architecture and the attention mechanism to prioritize important information during processing. Since running LLMs locally can be demanding, we discussed how to utilize APIs for efficient access, along with setting up OpenAI accounts for exploration. The OpenAI Playground emerged as a key environment for testing models, allowing us to fine-tune various parameters. Finally, we touched on integrating LLMs in Python, emphasizing the importance of structured output for reliable performance.
